Welcome to the Toronto Healthcare Outbreak Tracker:

Greetings and welcome to the Toronto Healthcare Outbreak Tracker, a comprehensive initiative aimed at shedding light on outbreaks within healthcare institutions across Toronto. In an era where health plays a pivotal role in our lives, understanding and monitoring the dynamics of outbreaks is more critical than ever.

About the Project:

At its core, this project serves as a centralized hub for accessing, analyzing, and comprehending data related to outbreaks in healthcare settings throughout the city. Leveraging a rich dataset meticulously curated from Toronto health authorities, our platform offers a user-friendly interface to explore and interpret trends, patterns, and crucial insights.

Why Outbreak Monitoring Matters:

Healthcare-associated outbreaks pose unique challenges, and their monitoring is essential for safeguarding public health. This project seeks to bridge the gap between data and understanding, empowering healthcare professionals, researchers, policymakers, and the public with actionable information to make informed decisions.

Scope and Objectives:

Focused on outbreaks within Toronto healthcare institutions, our project aims to provide a holistic view of the situation. Whether you're a healthcare professional seeking real-time insights or a concerned citizen wanting to stay informed, this platform caters to diverse needs.

Data Source:

Our data is sourced directly from authoritative health channels, ensuring reliability and accuracy. The data is from Toronto open data Toronto open data

How to Use the Dashboard:

Our dashboard is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly. The dashboard is divided into three main sections: the map, the charts, and the table. The map provides a visual representation of the data, allowing you to explore outbreaks by location. The charts offer a more in-depth analysis of the data, presenting key insights and trends. Finally, the table provides a comprehensive overview of the data, allowing you to filter and sort the data as needed.
